DM Bio

  • Lorphic Tales

    • Dnd
    • New

    I am Lorphic Tales and it’s an honor to me that you’re reading my bio. My journey with Dungeons and Dragons began with the experience of Larian Studio’s Baldur’s Gate 3. The rich lore, grandiose setting, and roleplaying experience piqued my interest into trying out the Table Top Roleplaying Experience with my local game store at TC’s Rockets, San Diego. Since then I’ve played an avid role as both a player and dungeon master in community events such as SDCC with West Coast Adventure’s Guild, Halloween with Kraken Dice, SD Rocket Con, and more. My passion for the game stems from the creativity the community creates and the role they play to make fantasy become reality. My goal in every session is to make people excited to play Dungeons & Dragons, feel like they belong, and maybe even walk away with new friends. I hope that you, the reader, are able to find that when playing Dungeons and Dragons. In whatever shape or form that it takes.

  • Angelo

    • Dnd
    • CoS

    Hey there gremlins, goblins, and ghouls! My name is Angelo and I've been in the D&D community for over a decade. Beginning in my middle school years, I have continued to create and explore vibrant and intricate stories for friends, family, and so so many others and I can only hope that I get to have you at one of my future campaigns! If you're looking for a DM who will spend hours on end, helping you craft your characters into an evolving and enriched world then bring some snacks and take a seat at my table!
    I have worked at RocketCon and plan on continuing to expand my reach to even more Cons in the future. The biggest milestone in the D&D space would be joining TCsRockets Adventures as this has truly kickstarted my passion to bringing campaigns to even more people! My style and flare is built off of roleplay and story first, followed closely by intricate and intense combat.

  • Eric

    • Starwars
    • Fantasy

    Eric has been running worlds and telling stories on and off since 1984. For the past eight years, he has been a full-time Dungeon Master, guiding players of all ages through multi-year campaigns, epic one-shots, and countless adventures. His tables are known for immersive roleplay, gritty realism, and worlds where the fantastic still makes sense. Eric focuses on player-led storytelling while ensuring that magic, combat, and choices feel tangible and impactful. His real-world experiences—climbing mountains, spelunking, trudging through hip-deep snow, and even medieval fighting in armor under the sun—fuel the authenticity of the stories he weaves. He currently runs games with TC Rockets, the West Coast Adventurers Guild, and has run D&D at some of the biggest cons, including Comic-Con, WonderCon, and Rocket-Con. He is also passionate about passing the torch onto a whole new generation of players, having led a year-long after-school program introducing 5th graders to D&D. For Eric, being a Dungeon Master is more than running a game—it is building worlds, sharing adventures, and creating unforgettable memories at the table.
